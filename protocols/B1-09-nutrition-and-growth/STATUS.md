  suspected child neglect / food-insecurity-driven failure.

  CMPA, severe behavioural feeding (multidisciplinary feeding clinic),
  vitamin D with bony deformity, B12 with persistent neuro

  female annually.** Departure from AAP universal-once-at-12m.
  Defended on Indian-context high prevalence. Re-check.

  Departure from WHO 3-month total. Defended on Indian-context
  relapse rates. Re-check.
